## Summary
- The strategy introduces the separation of a point-to-point integration into two distinct flows: producer and consumer.
- It uses a mandatory common language: the enterprise data model.
- It introduces two methods for producing enterprise information via enterprise endpoints: enterprise APIs and enterprise distribution channels.
- It includes building blocks to simplify design and implementation of integration flows: molecules and patterns.
- It includes automated deployment for the necessary infrastructure components.
