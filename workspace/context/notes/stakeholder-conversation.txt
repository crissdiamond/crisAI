Stakeholder Conversation Notes

Stakeholders asked whether Power BI can just connect to the spreadsheet directly.
They said speed is important, but they also want confidence that figures are consistent month to month.

Architecture concern raised:
If logic is implemented only in Power BI, it may be difficult to assure, reuse, or explain later.

Suggested compromise:
start with a simple ingestion route, keep transformations minimal, and document known data quality issues.
