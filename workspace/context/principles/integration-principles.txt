## Overarching Principles
Durable statements that align integration work with UCL’s broader institutional goals.

* **Secure by Design**: Cloud native organisation with inherent security, privacy, and compliance by design to reduce reputational risk.
* **Reskilled Workforce**: Focus on enabling modern, advanced capabilities rather than just providing operational functions.
* **Scalable, Optimised Estate**: An estate that scales based on business demand.
* **Everything as a Service**: Consumption as a service irrespective of delivery model, defined by clear objective criteria.
* **Sustainable Solutions**: Environmental, technical, and cost perspective sustainability to ensure supportability throughout the lifecycle.

## Strategic Principles
* **Cloud-Optimised**: Integrations are designed to run in cloud environments, reducing information transfer to only what is required.
* **Ease of Development and Maintenance**: Easing development effort and building solutions that adapt quickly to demand.
* **Reusable**: Guidelines and design patterns applied consistently across the enterprise to increase ROI and reduce duplication.
* **Clear Ownership**: Domain areas are accountable for the implementation, operation, and oversight of their interfaces.
