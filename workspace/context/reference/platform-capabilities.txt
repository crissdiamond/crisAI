Platform Capabilities Reference

The current data platform supports batch ingestion, curated data products, semantic models, and operational reporting.
The preferred ingestion route is through a controlled ingestion service rather than unmanaged direct uploads.

The platform is suitable for:
- structured operational data from enterprise systems
- scheduled ingestion of files
- publishing reusable curated datasets
- producing Power BI reports from governed data products

The platform is not currently positioned as a low-code spreadsheet automation tool.
Where Excel files are used as a source, they should be treated as data inputs and moved into a controlled data pipeline when the output becomes operational or recurring.

Relevant capability: Bronze landing, Silver staging, Silver curated, Gold data products.
