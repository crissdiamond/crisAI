## Implementation Phases
Note: This document reflects the historical roadmap established in September 2021.

### Phase 1: Initialisation & Early Adoption (Term 1, 21/22)
* Definition of patterns, standards, and processes for self-service.
* Platform Team training and onboarding.
* First integrations with early adopters (e.g., Student Records, Estates).

### Phase 2: Controlled Rollout (Academic Year 21/22)
* Go-live of first integrations (e.g., SITS to StarRez).
* Definition of training and rollout frameworks.

### Phase 3: ISD-wide Release (Academic Year 22/23 onwards)
* Full go-live and continual central support.
* Transition to a 3-year delivery roadmap for the Central Team.
