## Devolved Delivery Model
The strategy shifts from a centralised bottleneck to a federated model of "Empowered Product Teams."

## Roles and Responsibilities
### Central Team (Integration Services)
* **Platform Management**: Managing cloud-native components and tooling.
* **Design Guardrails**: Defining best practices and standards.
* **Consultancy & Collaboration**: Offering implementation overflow and guidance to product teams.

### Devolved Teams (Domain/Product Teams)
* **Full Lifecycle Management**: Design, build, test, release, operate, and decommission their own interfaces: producers (for master systems) and consumers (for data consumers or providers).
* **Data Ownership**: Responsible for access management and the quality of data flowing between applications.
* **Domain-level Oversight**: Includes roles such as Data Modellers, Solution Architects, and Business Analysts within the domain.

### Consumers
* **Citizen Integrators**: Students and researchers empowered to collect and connect data themselves via self-service tools.
