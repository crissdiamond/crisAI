System Landscape Reference

The institutional application landscape includes major enterprise systems, portfolio-owned applications, SaaS products, and local operational files.
Not every useful data source is an enterprise system.

A source can be accepted temporarily even if it is not technically mature, but the design must make ownership, lineage, quality, and retention explicit.

Excel and CSV files are common transitional sources.
They are acceptable for discovery or prototype reporting, but not ideal as long-term operational reporting sources without governance controls.
