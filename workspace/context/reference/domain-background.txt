Domain Background Reference

Several portfolios need reporting over operational processes that are currently tracked in local files.
These files often start as manual trackers but gradually become important sources for management decisions.

Common issues:
- business definitions are unclear
- ownership of local files is ambiguous
- data quality checks are manual
- reports are recreated differently by different teams
- lineage is difficult to explain

For architecture decisions, the key question is whether the use case is ad hoc analysis or a recurring operational reporting capability.
Recurring reporting should normally move towards a governed data product.
