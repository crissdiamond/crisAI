Existing Design Example: Prototype Reporting Route

A prototype reporting design allowed analysts to connect directly to local files for a short experiment.
The design included a clear expiry condition:
- if the report became recurring
- if it was shared beyond the immediate working group
- or if decisions depended on it

then the source had to move into a controlled ingestion and curation route.

This design is useful because it distinguishes exploratory analysis from operational reporting.
