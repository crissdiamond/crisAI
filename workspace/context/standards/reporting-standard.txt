Standard: Reporting Data Preparation

Recurring reports must not rely on undocumented transformations hidden only inside a report file.
Business rules, joins, filters, and calculations should be documented or implemented in controlled data preparation layers.

Power BI may be used for visualisation, exploration, and lightweight calculations, but should not be the only location where critical transformation logic exists.

A report is considered recurring if it is expected to be refreshed or used more than twice for operational decision-making.
