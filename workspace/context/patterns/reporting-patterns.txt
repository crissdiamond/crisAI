Reusable Pattern: Operational Reporting

For operational reporting, the preferred design is to separate data preparation from presentation.

Recommended pattern:
- source data is ingested into the platform
- transformations are performed in controlled pipelines
- quality checks are recorded before publishing
- a curated dataset or gold layer is exposed
- Power BI is used for presentation and user interaction

Anti-pattern:
- connecting Power BI directly to unmanaged local files
- embedding business rules only inside report visuals
- duplicating transformations in multiple reports

This pattern supports reuse and reduces hidden logic.
