Reusable Pattern: Data Ownership and Access

Every data product or reporting dataset should identify:
- accountable Data Owner
- operational Data Steward
- technical Data Custodian
- approved user groups
- access route
- escalation path for data quality issues

For local-file sources, ownership is often unclear.
The architecture should explicitly assign interim ownership before the source is used for recurring reporting.

Access to reports should be based on role or group membership rather than individually managed exceptions.
